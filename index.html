Biography

About Me

My name is Amani, a Nairobi-based photographer driven by a passion for adventure, food, expeditions, cars, and the beauty of nature. With a style rooted in natural light and close-up detail, I strive to capture the raw essence of every moment, turning fleeting experiences into timeless stories.

My Journey

From a young age, I loved drawing pictures, fascinated by the intricate details that art could reveal. That curiosity grew into a love for photography, where I discovered the power of the lens to preserve not just images, but emotions. Traveling across diverse landscapes has deepened my desire to document each moment, ensuring that every frame tells a story worth remembering.

My Work

I’ve had the privilege of working with individual clients, creating personalized collections that reflect their unique journeys. My photography has also been featured in Kenya’s most popular magazines, a recognition that continues to inspire me to push creative boundaries and share my vision with a wider audience.

My Philosophy

For me, photography is more than just capturing images—it’s about emotion and storytelling. Each photograph is a chapter in a larger narrative, a way to connect with others and inspire them through the beauty of everyday life and extraordinary adventures.
